Active Diet Plan

Macronutrient Targets

  • ●
    Protein Base: Target 120g daily. Focus on lean chicken breast, organic tofu, and fish rich in Omega-3s.
  • ●
    Carbohydrate Control: Target < 150g daily. Shift complex carbohydrates (quinoa, oats) to morning hours prior to scheduled aerobic activity.

Restricted Items

  • ●
    Avoid high sodium processed foods completely to mitigate Hypertension risks.
